About us

Quartz That Works for You

LUX is a specialized quartz slab manufacturer based in Vietnam, proudly serving importers, fabricators, and design-focused brands who need more than just standard supply. While many factories chase volume, we focus on precision, consistency, and relationships.

Our strength lies in offering a curated range of high-demand designs—Carrara, Calacatta, pure whites—and delivering them with batch-to-batch reliability and clear, honest communication.

Exclusive Quartz Development

Need a design matched to your best-selling SKU or a niche color developed for a private label? We offer fast prototyping and low minimum order quantities, without sacrificing quality or lead time. Every slab is backed by visual QC reports, professional packing, and a team that understands your priorities: consistency, transparency, and long-term partnership.

Whether you’re stocking a new warehouse, fulfilling project orders, or building your own brand, we adapt to your workflow—not the other way around. You don’t need the biggest factory—you need the most responsive and dependable one.

Let us help you deliver more value with less friction.

Innovation, precision, customer satisfaction, and sustainability form the pillars of Lux Quartz Vietnam.

Discover the Lux Quartz difference. Whether you’re looking for bulk purchases or specific customizations, we are ready to meet your needs with precision and excellence.

As gentle as fluffy clouds floating in the deep blue sky, Lux Quartz's LQ-504 brings you a pristine and luxurious beauty. This design will be the perfect highlight for your modern space, helping you to affirm your sophisticated and classy taste.
