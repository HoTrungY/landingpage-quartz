    LUX Prefabricated Quartz Countertops Are Ideal For

     

    1. Bathroom Vanity Tops

    Quartz is renowned for its durability and elegant finish. Unlike marble or granite vanity tops, LUX offers a non-porous surface that is resistant to stains and scratches, making them ideal for any bathroom.

    Lux custom bathroom vanity tops come in various sizes: 36-inch vanity tops, 48-inch vanity tops, 60-inch vanity tops, 72-inch vanity tops, and more.

    Reach out to us promptly to share the specifics of your custom vanity top requirements.

    2. Kitchen Countertop & Islands

    LUX prefab quartz countertops are strong, durable, stain and scratch-resistant, and highly safe. Besides quartz bathroom vanity tops, quartz is an excellent choice for kitchen countertops and island tops. LUX prefabricated quartz countertops offer a pristine white surface that is not commonly found in prefab granite countertops, making them a top-notch option for your kitchen.

    3. Window sills and Threshold

    The hospitality industry or multifamily projects such as hotels, restaurants, and resorts, usually require a threshold, especially for main and bathroom entries.

    When it comes to design, color, and size, LUX provides a diverse range of options for quartz thresholds and window sills, ensuring optimal design and installation flexibility. Choose from a variety of sizes, such as 2×24, 4×36, 6×72, and more, with multiple widths and edge profiles available.

    4. Customized Furniture

    When it comes to quartz worktops for your custom furniture projects, look no further. Reach out to us today and discover the wide range of options available in our LUX Prefab Quartz Collection. Whether you require quartz surfaces for dining tables, end tables, or coffee tables, we have got you covered.
