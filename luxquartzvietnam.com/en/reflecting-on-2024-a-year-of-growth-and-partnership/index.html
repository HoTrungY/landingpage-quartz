Reflecting on 2024: A Year of Growth and Partnership

30/12/2024     Lux Quartz 1

As we stand at the threshold of 2025, we at Lux are taking a moment to reflect on what has been a year of growth, challenges overcome, and partnerships strengthened. Throughout 2024, we’ve witnessed significant expansion in our global reach, with our premium quartz slabs finding their way to more international destinations than ever before.

  1. A Journey of Resilience and Vision
  2. Key Achievements in 2024
  3. Looking Forward to 2025
  4. Gratitude and Partnership
  5. Stay Connected

A Journey of Resilience and Vision

Our story began in 2020, during one of the most challenging periods in recent global history – the COVID-19 pandemic. While the world faced unprecedented uncertainties, we laid the foundation for what would become a remarkable journey in the engineered stone industry. The chart below illustrates our transformation from a company born in the midst of global challenges to a thriving manufacturer of premium quartz slabs and prefabs:

This visualization captures our inspiring journey: from our establishment during the COVID-19 pandemic in 2020, through our breakthrough first year of exports in 2021—a milestone marked by our expansion into producing quartz prefabs to meet diverse market demands—navigating market adjustments in 2022, achieving a strong recovery in 2023, and culminating in our most successful year yet in 2024, where we’ve more than doubled our operational scale compared to our initial year of exports. This growth isn’t just about numbers – it represents the resilience of our team, the trust our clients place in our quality and service, and our unwavering commitment to excellence even in the face of global challenges. In just four years, we’ve achieved significant milestones that underlie our commitment to growth and innovation. We are proud of our journey and remain steadfast in our dedication to continuous development in the years ahead

Key Achievements in 2024

Brand Evolution – Our New Identity: 2024 marked a significant step in our brand’s evolution with the unveiling of our New Logo. Designed to symbolize growth, trust, and our forward-thinking vision, the refreshed logo represents who we are today and our aspirations for the future.

The “Waves of Life” Collection Launch: This newest collection embodies the harmony of design and functionality, offering nature-inspired patterns and elegant styles that elevate spaces to new heights. Each piece reflects our dedication to craftsmanship and innovation. 

Adapting to Market Trends: 2024 has been a year of evolution in the engineered stone industry. We’ve observed and adapted to emerging trends, including:

  •  Growing demand for sustainable manufacturing practices
  •  Increased preference for sophisticated, nature-inspired designs
  • Rising focus on product durability and longevity
  • Expanding applications in both residential and commercial projects

Looking Forward to 2025

With 2025 on the horizon, we are inspired to set new benchmarks in the industry. Our vision for the coming year includes

  • Product Innovation: Expanding our range with new designs and technologies
  • Service Excellence: Enhancing our customer support and project consultation services
  • Partnership Growth: Strengthening existing relationships and building new connections
  • Industry Leadership: Contributing to sustainable practices and industry advancement

Gratitude and Partnership

None of our achievements would have been possible without our valued customers and partners. Your continued trust in Lux has been the cornerstone of our growth. We’re deeply grateful for every opportunity to serve you, for every project entrusted to us, and for every relationship we’ve built and strengthened throughout the year.
