The Logistics Journey of Artificial Quartz: From Quartz Surface Factory in Vietnam to Distributors

04/04/2025     Lux Quartz 1

Behind every beautifully installed quartz countertop is a journey most people never see. From raw materials to polished slabs, from factory to warehouse, there’s a detailed logistics process that ensures each quartz slab arrives safely, on time, and in perfect condition.

At Lux Quartz Vietnam, we believe that quality doesn’t stop at production—it extends all the way to delivery. As a quartz surface factory in Vietnam, we work closely with our partners to deliver not just great-looking slabs, but also a smooth, dependable supply chain experience.

Here’s a closer look at how artificial quartz from our factory makes its way across the world to distributors, wholesalers, and fabrication shops.

  2. Smart and Safe Packing
  3. Loading the Containers
  4. Paperwork and Shipping
  5.  Arrival and Unloading

  7. Final Words

From the Factory Floor

Everything begins in our manufacturing facility in Vietnam, where advanced machinery and skilled workers come together to produce high-quality quartz slabs. Each piece goes through a strict quality control process to ensure the color, pattern, thickness, and polish meet our standards.

This is especially important for high-demand items like Calacatta Quartz from Vietnam, which are prized for their luxurious veining and clean white base. Before anything leaves our plant, it’s double-checked to ensure consistency and durability.

Smart and Safe Packing

Once the slabs are approved, we move to the packing stage. Every slab is wrapped and packed into heavy-duty wooden bundles, built to handle long-distance travel. These bundles are designed to minimize movement and protect edges and surfaces from any kind of damage.

Distributors who buy factory direct quartz expect the products to arrive in excellent condition—and that’s exactly what we deliver. The care we take at this step helps our clients save time and money on rework or returns.

Loading the Containers

Our team loads the slabs into containers using forklifts fitted with special boom attachments. This allows us to move the wooden bundles efficiently without risking damage. Each container is loaded strategically to make the most of the space while keeping everything secure for the journey ahead.

Whether it’s a small mixed load or a full container of a single color, our shipping team ensures that everything is prepared with precision. That’s one reason many clients prefer to work with us as their main quartz stone supplier.

Paperwork and Shipping

We know that international shipping can get complicated, especially when customs paperwork isn’t done correctly. That’s why our logistics team handles all the necessary documents—packing lists, invoices, certificates, and shipping papers—with care and accuracy.

Our dedicated export team provides:

  • Complete material certification including NSF compliance documentation
  • Origin certificates
  • Pre-cleared customs forms with proper HS codes and material descriptions
  • Digital and physical copies of all paperwork accessible through email

We also coordinate closely with freight forwarders and shipping lines to make sure your container leaves Vietnam on time. Whether you’re importing for a large warehouse or a boutique design studio, we’ve got your back.

 Arrival and Unloading

Depending on your location, the ocean freight journey can take a few weeks. Working with premium shipping partners, we maintain predictable delivery schedules for containers departing from our Danang, Vietnam facility:

  • North America (West Coast): 25-30 days transit time
  • North America (East Coast): 35-45 days transit time
  • Europe: 30-35 days transit time
  • Australia: 15-20 days transit time
  • Middle East (UAE, Qatar): 18-22 days transit time
  • Japan & South Korea: 7-10 days transit time
  • Southeast Asia (Singapore, Malaysia): 5-7 days transit time

These estimates account for typical ocean freight schedules, port processing times, and potential transshipment where required. Our logistics team provides precise tracking and updates throughout the journey, ensuring you can plan your inventory management and customer deliveries with confidence.

Once the container arrives, most of our clients use forklifts to unload the bundles quickly and safely. We pack the slabs in a logical order to match the invoice, so it’s easier for you to sort and store once it arrives.

If you’re working with quartz stone wholesale, time is everything. Our goal is to get your inventory into your hands as quickly and efficiently as possible—so you can focus on serving your customers.

Why Work with Lux Quartz Vietnam?

As one of the top quartz stone suppliers in the region, we offer more than just quality slabs. We offer a streamlined supply chain, responsive service, and a commitment to your success. Our clients appreciate the direct connection to a quartz stone fabrication team in Vietnam, which allows for better communication, lower costs, and more reliable planning.

What sets our logistics process apart from competitors:

  • Proprietary tracking system allowing real-time monitoring of your shipment across every transportation phase
  • Dedicated logistics assistant assigned to each client account, providing personalized updates and rapid problem resolution
  • Flexible consolidation options that allow mixing different quartz varieties in a single container while maintaining perfect organization

Whether you’re looking for everyday bestsellers or signature colors like Calacatta Quartz from Vietnam, we’re ready to help you grow your business with confidence.

Final Words

The logistics journey of artificial quartz is just as important as the product itself. At Lux Quartz Vietnam, we take pride in handling both with care. From production to packaging, from paperwork to port, every step is managed with precision.
