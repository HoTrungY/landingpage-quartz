1. What is artificial stone?

Artificial stone is a material crafted mainly from quartz powder or sand, glass powder, resin-based adhesive, colorants, and additives. Generally, quartz makes up about 90%-93% of the material, while the rest comprises binding agents and additional elements.

LUX artificial stone finds extensive use in interior design and architecture, including flooring, kitchen countertops, pillars, wall embellishments, and various other applications.
